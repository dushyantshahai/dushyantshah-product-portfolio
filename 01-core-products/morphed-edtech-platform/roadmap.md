# Product Roadmap — MorphEd

## What's Been Built (v1 — Current)

MorphEd v1 is live and in early access. It delivers the core teacher workflow end-to-end.

### Shipped Features
- **Syllabus ingestion:** PDF and DOCX upload with text-layer and basic OCR support
- **Topic hierarchy extraction:** Auto-parsed unit/chapter/sub-topic tree using Claude structured output
- **RAG-based MCQ generation:** Context-grounded questions with 4 options, correct answer, and explanation
- **Topic-level filtering:** Generate MCQs for specific chapters or sub-topics
- **Quiz configuration:** Question count (5–50), difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard / Mixed)
- **MCQ review & edit:** Inline editing, per-question regeneration
- **Export:** PDF download (question paper + separate answer key)
- **Try-before-register flow:** No account required for first session
- **Teacher dashboard:** Saved syllabi, quiz history

### Known v1 Gaps
- No student-facing mode
- No LMS integrations
- PDF export only (no Google Form, no direct sharing)
- English-only (Hindi-medium syllabi partially supported)
- No analytics for teachers (how did students perform?)

## What's Next (v2 — Planned: Q3–Q4 2025)

Prioritisation rationale: v2 features are chosen to (1) increase teacher retention by expanding utility, and (2) unlock the student-side of the flywheel — which is required for the B2B institutional pitch to become compelling.

### v2 Feature Set

**1. Student Practice Mode**
Students access quizzes generated by their teacher via a shareable link. No app download required — works in browser. Students see instant score + per-topic breakdown after submitting.

*Why now:* Enables the network effect. A teacher who can share quizzes with students gets more value from MorphEd. Student engagement data creates the analytics teachers need to justify the tool to their institutions.

*RICE Score: 9.2* — High reach (every quiz creates a shareable link), high impact (activates the student side of the flywheel), medium confidence, low effort (read-only student view requires no auth complexity).

---

**2. Teacher Performance Dashboard**
After students complete quizzes, teachers see: class average, topic-level weak spots, individual student scores (if students logged in), and suggested "focus topics" for the next class.

*Why now:* This is the feature that converts MorphEd from a "time-saving tool" to a "teaching intelligence tool." It's the feature most likely to drive institutional sales conversations.

*RICE Score: 7.8*

---

**3. Adaptive Difficulty**
MCQs are tagged with difficulty and topic. When students practise, difficulty adjusts based on performance — students who ace Easy questions get Medium ones next. Uses a simple IRT-inspired scoring model, not full adaptive testing.

*Why now:* Differentiates MorphEd from static quiz tools. Increases student session length and return rate. Required for the "AI study companion" positioning.

*RICE Score: 6.4*

---

**4. Google Form Export**
One-click export of generated MCQs to a pre-populated Google Form, ready to share with students via Google Classroom.

*Why now:* Highest-requested feature from pilot teachers (8 of 18 mentioned it unprompted). Reduces the friction between MCQ generation and distribution.

*RICE Score: 8.1*

---

**5. Hindi-Medium Syllabus Support**
Full multilingual support for Hindi-language syllabi, including Hindi question generation. Requires prompt engineering, language detection, and output validation updates.

*Why now:* ~40% of Indian state board syllabi are in Hindi. Unlocking Hindi opens MorphEd to a significantly larger teacher market.

*RICE Score: 7.1*

## What's After v2 (v3 — 12–18 Month Horizon)

These features are directionally committed but not scoped. They depend on v2 usage data and institutional traction.

**LMS Integrations (Moodle, Canvas, Google Classroom)**
Enables direct quiz sync into institution-managed learning environments. Required for the enterprise/university sales motion.

**Subjective Question Generation**
Extend beyond MCQs to short-answer and essay prompts. Opens MorphEd to humanities and language courses where MCQs are less appropriate.

**Spaced Repetition Engine**
Students are reminded to revisit topics they struggled with, timed using spaced repetition intervals (SM-2 algorithm). Transforms MorphEd from a quiz tool into a study system.

**Multi-Teacher Institutional Accounts**
Role-based access for HODs and department coordinators. Shared syllabus libraries, usage analytics across the department, and centralised question bank management.

**Proctor Mode**
Time-limited, browser-locked quiz delivery for formal assessments. Required for institutions that want to use MorphEd for graded internal exams (not just practice).

## Prioritisation Framework Used: RICE

**Reach:** How many teachers or students does this feature impact per quarter?
**Impact:** On a 1–3 scale, how much does this move the NSM (MCQs generated per active teacher per week) or retention?
**Confidence:** How confident are we in the estimates? Based on pilot data, interviews, or assumption?
**Effort:** Engineering weeks to ship, including design and QA.

`RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort`

All v2 features were scored independently and reviewed against the v1 usage data from the pilot cohort before being sequenced.
